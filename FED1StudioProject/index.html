About Us

Camping People was founded with the idea that not everyone has the ability, skill, knowledge, or otherwise opportunity to experience a weekend outdoors. Our classes are a cross between technical skill training including tying knots, setting up shelter, or finding food and a classic fun outdoor adventure!
