We offer six workout packages, each with a difficulty level from beginner to advanced, with intermediate being the middle ground course. All beginner classes are on Mondays and Tuesdays, all intermediate courses are on Wednesdays and Thursdays, and all advanced classes are on Fridays and Saturdays. Each session takes you from warmup to cooldown and is two hours long.
